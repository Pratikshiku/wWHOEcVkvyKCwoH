## Red Revolution Cultural Relic Collection Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/41733e4fcf93488ba3dbb3a915fbf008/ghb20250918) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (any of the listed software can be used)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Functional Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users, staff, administrators.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Forum
5. Announcements & News
6. Cultural Relics
7. Personal Center
8. Cultural Relic Information
9. Cultural Relic Collections
10. Cultural Relic Comments

**Staff Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Cultural Relic Management
6. Cultural Relic Comment Management
7. Cultural Relic Comment Management
8. Forum Management
9. Announcement & News Management


**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
3. Administrator Management
4. Staff Management
5. User Management
6. Cultural Relic Management
7. Cultural Relic Comment Management
8. Cultural Relic Collection Management
9. Announcement Type Management
10. Cultural Relic Type Management
11. Forum Management
12. Announcement & News Management
13. Carousel Management
